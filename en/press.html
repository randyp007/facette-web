About Facette

Facette is a calm, ad-free brain-training app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac — five minutes for your mind. Each day brings a short, adaptive session drawn from 20+ games across 12 cognitive areas, with gentle feedback and forgiving streaks that survive a missed day. It's local-first by design: no account, no ads, no tracking, no analytics. Progress stays on your device, with optional private iCloud sync. Made in Linz, Austria, by independent developer Andreas Putzinger.

What makes it different

  • Ad-free, forever — no banners, no sponsored games, in the free version or with Pro.
  • Local-first and private: no account, no tracking, no analytics SDK, no ATT prompt.
  • Adaptive difficulty keeps you at the edge of challenge — roughly 80% flow, never crushing.
  • Non-punishing feedback and forgiving streaks — amber, not alarm-red — so a missed day isn't a failure.
  • 20+ short games across 12 cognitive areas, plus a per-domain skill radar that shows where you really stand.
  • Universal Purchase for iPhone, iPad, and Mac; optional private iCloud sync.

A note from the maker

I built Facette because my own attention felt scattered — pulled in ten directions by feeds engineered to keep me scrolling. I wanted the opposite: a small, calm ritual that gives something back instead of taking. Five quiet minutes for my mind in the morning, then on with the day. So Facette has no ads, no tracking, and no tricks to keep you hooked. It adapts to you, forgives a missed day, and keeps everything on your device. I made the app I wished existed — and I still open it every morning myself.

— Andreas Putzinger, Linz
