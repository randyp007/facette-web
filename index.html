What it is

A quiet daily habit for your mind

Facette is brain training without the noise. Each day gives you a short, adaptive session — long enough to matter, short enough to keep. The games span twelve cognitive areas such as attention, memory, reasoning and processing speed, and gently adjust to your level so you are always working at the right edge of challenge. No leaderboards to chase, no dark patterns, no pressure. Just five minutes.

What makes Facette different

What's different

Facette isn't here to make noise. No account, no ads, no tracking — everything stays on your device. The games adapt to you and stay calm and fair, with no dark patterns and no pressure. And we don't promise miracles: Facette is a good, challenging daily ritual for your mind — not a magic pill for the brain.

What Facette does

  • Helps you get measurably better at exactly these exercises (near transfer).
  • Builds a calm, consistent daily practice you can actually keep.
  • Adapts to your level so training stays appropriately challenging.
  • Respects your attention, your time and your privacy.

What we don't promise

  • No claims about raising your IQ.
  • No prevention, treatment or cure for dementia or any condition.
  • No medical or therapeutic claims — Facette is not a medical device.
  • No promise of broad, real-world "far transfer" benefits.
